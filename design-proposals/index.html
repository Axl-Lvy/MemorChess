What's new in this revision

  • Light theme rebuilt as futuristic. Out: warm cream paper. In: cool ice white with blue undertones (#EEF2F7 bg, #0A0E16 ink), tech-grid background, electric cyan #00B8D4 as secondary accent, neon orange #FF5B26 popping against ice. Cards now cast cyan-tinted hard shadows. Board squares are cool slate-blue.
  • Training screen ruthlessly simplified. Removed FSRS card, 30-day heatmap, repertoire health bars, states preview, and the session stats banner. Desktop is now a single centered column with one large counter + bigger board. Mobile keeps just board, turn pill, action buttons.
  • Brand mark, board accent stripe, and topbar underline now use a cyan→orange gradient with a soft glow — same on dark, more pronounced on light.
  • Primary buttons in light mode cast a soft orange glow; "Your move" tag glows too.
  • .trail component (kept from prior revision) handles long move lines: horizontal scroll, fade-edge mask, current-move auto-snap, opening-name pin on left, PGN button that opens a full notation overlay.

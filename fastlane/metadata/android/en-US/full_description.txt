MemorChess helps you learn and retain chess openings the way Anki helps you learn
anything else: with spaced repetition. Instead of flipping through static
flashcards, you practice your lines directly on an interactive chessboard, so you
memorize real moves on a real board.

Features:

* Interactive chessboard for exploring and practicing opening lines
* Spaced repetition scheduling (FSRS) that resurfaces the lines you are about to forget
* Track your progress and focus review on the moves you find tricky
* Import public Lichess studies by URL to build your repertoire quickly
* Browse the Lichess opening explorer to see how positions are played
* Works fully offline; your repertoire is stored locally on your device

MemorChess is free and open source software, released under the GNU General Public
License v3.0.
